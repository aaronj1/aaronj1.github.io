About Me: Greetings! I'm Aaron and have a Bachelor's Degree in Cartography & GIS. I enjoy everything map-related, and have 2+ years of experience creating both static (paper) and interactive (digital) maps. Additionally, I'm familiar with Python, Javascript, and HTML programming.
